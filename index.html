IT Project

Overview

My idea for an IT project is a to create a streaming platform or an addition to a pre-existing platform that is more friendly for the viewers than the ones already out there like twitch, Facebook, you tube and others. The platform will be more friendly by allowing more viewer interaction and give more choices to the viewer as opposed to the streamer.

Motivation

My motivation for making such a platform is my experiences watching streamers on the existing platforms. For example, a lot of the time when I watch dotA 2 on twitch there are often arguments where the streamer should put their face cam or which statistics, like biggest donors or subscribers to show on the screen. My idea is to make a platform that allows the viewer to make these decisions in order to give everyone the best viewing experience possible.

description

The streaming platform will allow people to stream whatever they want whist giving the viewers the ability to make more decisions. Amongst the decisions that viewers will be able to make, firstly there will be the ability to move location and change size of the streamers face cam in order that they could choose which parts of the gameplay or whatever is being streamed they don’t mind being blocked or even putting it over part of the chat or off to the side. Another choice to give the viewers is where to put things like the subscriber stats or donation stats that often also usually block part of the screen. The issue with this is that often streamers want their viewers to see things in order to persuade the viewers to do thing, for example they show the donation stats to convince you to donate, so therefore the streamers will be able to choose things you have to have somewhere on the screen. For example, the streamer can choose that you must display the top 3 subscriber stats or put a minimum size on their face camera and other things like that.

Outcome

If the project is successful it will solve the issues by allowing the viewer to make the choices so that there will no longer be any complaints while at the same time giving the streamer some choices to make sure that they don’t lose out so everyone ends up happy.
